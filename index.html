1. Introduction

Typelo is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal or sensitive data. It is designed to be fully offline, ad-free, and permission-free to offer a safe and simple user experience.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Typelo does not collect or transmit any of the following:

3. Permissions

Typelo does not request or require any runtime permissions.

4. Third-Party Services

Typelo does not use any third-party SDKs, libraries, or services that may collect user data (e.g., Firebase, Analytics, AdMob, etc.).

5. Children’s Privacy

Typelo is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. The app contains no ads, no external links, and no in-app purchases.

6. Security

All interactions and features operate entirely offline. No data is ever sent to external servers, ensuring complete security and privacy.
